## 📌 One to Watch

The Anthropic-Pentagon clash is a bellwether for a defining tension in the AI era: the conflict between safety-focused AI developers trying to maintain usage guardrails and a military-industrial complex eager to deploy frontier models at speed, with or without consent. OpenAI's simultaneous $110B fundraise and Pentagon deal — contrasted with Anthropic's public standoff and the reported unauthorized use of Claude in Iran strikes — reveals two divergent playbooks emerging among leading AI labs. As the US government cements AI's role in kinetic military operations, expect intense pressure on every major lab to either negotiate terms with the DoD or face being sidelined. The next 90 days will likely force clearer public stances from Google DeepMind, Meta AI, and others on military use policies — making AI governance in national security one of the most consequential policy battlegrounds of 2026.
