## 📌 One to Watch

The deployment of AI in Operation Epic Fury marks a genuine inflection point — not just a policy debate, but a live demonstration of AI in lethal military operations at scale. Watch for how this accelerates the fracturing of the global AI governance landscape: the U.S. is now simultaneously running the most permissive federal AI policy in the Western world while actively deploying AI in active combat, even as its own intra-party coalition cracks over who gets to regulate the technology. The FDA's breakthrough designation for RecovryAI and the White House vs. GOP-state-lawmakers standoff on the same day suggest we're entering a phase where AI regulation is being litigated simultaneously across defense, healthcare, and state vs. federal jurisdictions — with no unified framework in sight. Companies and investors should expect a messy, fragmented regulatory environment for the next 12–18 months, with sector-specific rules emerging faster than any horizontal AI law.
