## 🚀 What's New

- **OpenAI Closes Record $110B Funding Round at $840B Valuation** — OpenAI has finalized a landmark $110 billion funding round — the largest in private tech history — valuing the ChatGPT maker at up to $840 billion. The round includes $30B from SoftBank, $30B from NVIDIA, and $50B from Amazon, and is paired with a new strategic partnership bringing OpenAI's Frontier platform to AWS with stateful agent infrastructure. The deal dwarfs OpenAI's prior $41B raise and signals an escalating arms race in AI investment. ([OpenAI Blog](https://openai.com/index/scaling-ai-for-everyone) · [OpenAI Blog – Amazon Partnership](https://openai.com/index/amazon-partnership) · [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/27/open-ai-funding-round-amazon.html))

- **Anthropic vs. Pentagon: High-Stakes Standoff Over AI Safety in Military Use** — A deepening dispute between Anthropic and the Trump administration's Pentagon has escalated into a potential severance of government contracts, with the Defense Department demanding unrestricted access to Claude models. Anthropic's Pentagon version of Claude was built with restrictions barring use in mass surveillance of Americans and fully autonomous weapons systems — guardrails the DOD wants removed. The standoff is being watched as a defining moment for how AI safety principles will be tested against national security imperatives. ([Anthropic Blog](https://www.anthropic.com/news/statement-department-of-war) · [The New York Times](https://www.nytimes.com/2026/02/27/technology/defense-department-anthropic-ai-safety.html) · [The Atlantic](https://www.theatlantic.com/ideas/2026/02/hegseth-anthropic-dispute-ai/686150/))

Shares surged more than 20% in after-hours trading, though analysts raised concerns about operational continuity. The move marks one of the most explicit and large-scale corporate acknowledgments that AI is actively displacing white-collar jobs. ([Associated Press](https://apnews.com/article/block-dorsey-layoffs-ai-jobs-18e00a0b278977b0a87893f55e3db7bb) · [Reuters](https://www.msn.com/en-gb/money/other/dorseys-blunt-ai-warning-sharpens-debate-over-jobs-and-profits/ar-AA1XdZl2) · [American Banker](https://www.americanbanker.com/payments/news/block-replacing-40-of-its-staff-with-ai))

## 📌 One to Watch

Today's news draws a sharp fault line between AI's explosive capital accumulation and its growing governance crisis. OpenAI's $110B raise — backed by the very chip and cloud giants it depends on — cements a tight oligopoly at the frontier of AI development, where infrastructure leverage is becoming as important as model quality. Meanwhile, the Anthropic-Pentagon standoff previews a coming reckoning: as governments demand unconditional access to AI systems for defense applications, safety-focused labs will face existential pressure to either compromise their principles or forfeit lucrative government contracts. Watch whether other frontier labs (Google DeepMind, Meta AI) are forced to publicly declare where they stand on autonomous weapons and surveillance use cases — the Anthropic precedent may force everyone's hand.
